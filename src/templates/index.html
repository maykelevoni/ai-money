About

About PostForge

PostForge is an independent discovery platform that helps people find quality apps, software tools and trial offers. We aggregate and recommend offers from third-party providers and advertising partners. When you choose to sign up for or try a recommended offer, we may earn a referral commission — at no extra cost to you.

We only feature legitimate, non-incentivized offers and we are committed to a clean, transparent browsing experience. PostForge is not affiliated with the providers it recommends unless explicitly stated.
