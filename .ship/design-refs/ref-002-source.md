# PCMag Review Page Pattern

## Layout Structure
1. Product name + rating (X/5 "Excellent") — top of page
2. Author byline + date + social sharing
3. "The Bottom Line" verdict box
4. Pros & Cons
5. Pricing section
6. Feature-by-feature sections
7. Final verdict box with rating + recommendation + CTA

## Rating Display
- Numerical score (4.0/5.0) with label ("Excellent")
- Displayed prominently in header
- Repeated in final verdict box

## Verdict Box
- Product icon + name + score
- One concise recommendation sentence
- "GET IT NOW" CTA button
- Related product suggestions below

## CTA Pattern
- "See It" buttons after rating card
- "See It" within feature sections
- "GET IT NOW" in final verdict box
- Affiliate disclosure clearly stated near CTAs

## Trust Signals
- Author bio + expertise credentials
- Editorial methodology link
- "65 EXPERTS, 44 YEARS, 43K+ REVIEWS" badge
- Rating scale definitions

## Colors
- White background
- Star rating in yellow/gold
- Green/teal for positive elements
- Clear button styling (outlined + filled variants)
