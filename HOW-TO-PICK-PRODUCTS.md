# How to Pick Good Digistore24 Products

You hand-pick a few products, paste their IDs into the system, the system does the rest.
Picking well is the most important 5 minutes — a bad product burns ad money. Use this checklist.

## On the Digistore24 marketplace, look at these numbers

| What you see | What it means | What you want |
|---|---|---|
| **Earnings / Cart (€/$ per cart)** | How much an affiliate earns per visitor sent | HIGHER = better |
| **Commission %** | Your cut of each sale | 50%+ (digital products are usually 50–70%) |
| **Cancellation rate** | How many buyers refund/cancel | LOW. Under 10% good, under 5% great |
| **Stars / popularity** | Overall quality rating | More stars = safer |
| **Sales rank** | How well it sells vs others | Higher rank = proven seller |

## ✅ GOOD product = pick it
- Commission 50% or more
- Healthy earnings-per-cart (compare against others in the same niche)
- Low cancellation rate (few refunds)
- Has real sales history (NOT brand new with empty stats)
- **Auto-accepts affiliates** ← REQUIRED, or the system can't promote it hands-off
- Normal-looking sales page that actually loads (real video or page)
- Price that a stranger clicking an ad would impulse-buy: roughly **$30–$90**

## ⚠️ WARNINGS — AVOID these
- **High cancellation/refund rate (over ~15%)** → angry buyers, you lose commissions
- **Brand-new product, no stats** → unproven, likely a dud
- **Needs manual approval** → breaks the "hands-off" part, skip it
- **Price too high ($300+)** → hard to sell to cold ad traffic
- **Scammy claims** → "lose 30 lbs in a week", "guaranteed $5000/day", "miracle cure" → refunds + bans
- **Grey/banned niches** → gambling, adult, crypto-get-rich, payday loans → your engine blocks these anyway, and they get accounts banned

## Best niches for PropellerAds (cheap push/pop traffic)
Broad mass-market impulse offers convert best:
**weight loss / health, survival/preparedness, relationships, beauty, spirituality, make-money (clean only).**
Avoid narrow B2B or anything that needs a lot of thinking before buying.

## The system's safety net
After you paste IDs, the engine pulls each product's LIVE stats (commission, earnings/visitor,
cancellation rate, conversion) and automatically **drops any that fail the rules above** before
spending ad money. So if a bad one slips through, it gets caught. Your job is just the first pick.
